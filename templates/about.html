EOSDIS Usage Based Discovery

Who are We?

We are a team under NASA's Earth Observing System Data and Information System looking to improve search relevancy for
earth science data through the concept of usage-based discovery.



What is Usage-Based Discovery?

Instead of traditional methods for identifying datasets of interest through specific dataset features, quality data
is identified through the way in which it is seen and used in real-world applications. This can also be interpreted as social
data discovery, where essentially discovering this data is a collaborative process across the general community.
